## 🔬 About Me | Sobre Mim

- 🎓 **MSc student & researcher @ UFVJM** — Department of Agronomy
- 🧬 Focus: **soil-microbiome metagenomics**, microbial ecology & diversity
- 🌿 Applied to **phytoremediation** of herbicides and heavy metals (barium, zinc, sulfentrazone, metolachlor)
- 📊 I build **reproducible pipelines** for amplicon analysis: alpha/beta-diversity, ordination, **LEfSe** biomarkers, correlation networks
- 🐍📈 Working languages: **Python** and **R**
- 📍 Based in **Diamantina, Minas Gerais** — a UNESCO World Heritage city
- 📝 Published in the *Journal of Xenobiotics* (see below)

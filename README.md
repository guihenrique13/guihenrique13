## 💼 Experience | Experiência

### 🧬 Sequencing & Molecular Markers Intern — **GDM** (Cambé–PR) · Jan–Apr 2025
High-throughput genomics supporting GDM's **multi-crop molecular breeding
program** (soybean, maize, sorghum, wheat, sunflower):

- **Next-generation sequencing** on **Illumina, Ion Torrent and MGI T7** — genomic
  library preparation, quality control and preliminary data analysis in a
  high-throughput environment
- **High-throughput SNP genotyping** with **TaqMan** and **KASP** assays
  (endpoint PCR & qPCR on QuantStudio 3 / FLUOstar) for molecular-marker projects
- **Large-scale data traceability & QC** monitoring (**LGC Kraken**) across
  ~40,000 samples/day on automated platforms (Oktopure, Replikator, Hydrocycler, Solex)
- **Data monitoring & dashboards** with **Grafana** and **Elasticsearch**
